The New Directions in the Study of the Mind will fund a number of smaller research projects as part of this initiative. Philosophers, psychologists and neuroscientists are invited to apply for funding for these projects.

There will be two calls for proposals. The first call is now open, with a deadline for expressions of interest of 31 July 2015. The second will be in 2016.

Proposals will be evaluated by the project team and a selection of external experts.

Here are the details for those interested in applying for funding. Applications can be made on the University of Cambridge Faculty of Philosophy applications portal.
